From Classic to Rock

Project Paul Ya is a unique music initiative.

The project ranges from classical to rock music, offering a new interpretation of classical pieces with a rock edge.

This inventive approach showcases a distinctive conceptual vision, creativity in arrangements, and a standout presentation.
